Problem: Certifying a plumber takes weeks, costs £500, and requires an in-person assessor. There are 250k+ unfilled trade jobs in the UK alone because certification can't scale.

Solution: The worker uploads a video of themselves doing a task. AI vision assesses technique, safety, and result quality. Certificate issued in minutes, not weeks.

Customer: Training providers, recruitment agencies, and employers hiring tradespeople. They pay per assessment. Workers can also self-serve.
